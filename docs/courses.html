Course Title: Certificate III in Information, Digital Media, and Technology (ICT30111) Description: This vocational qualification offers foundational knowledge and skills in information technology, digital media, and related areas. It covers topics including computer hardware and software, networking, web development, multimedia design, and digital content creation. Students acquire practical skills essential for entry-level roles in the IT industry, including troubleshooting, basic programming, and digital media production.

 

Course Title: Managing Enterprise Virtual Computing Environments (ICTNWK527) Description: This course, ICTNWK533, focuses on the management of enterprise virtual computing environments. It explores various aspects of setting up and maintaining virtualized infrastructure to meet the demands of large-scale enterprises. Students will delve into virtualization technologies such as hypervisors, virtual machines (VMs), and containers, along with learning management tools and techniques for monitoring and optimizing performance.

 

Course Title: Designing and Deploying Scalable Network Solutions (ICTNWK731) Description: ICTNWK777 focuses on designing and deploying network solutions capable of scaling to meet the evolving demands of enterprise environments. Students delve into advanced networking concepts, including virtualization, software-defined networking (SDN), and network function virtualization (NFV). They learn to design, implement, and manage scalable network architectures to support the growth and flexibility required by modern enterprises.

 

Course Title: Implementing Enterprise Cloud Infrastructure (ICTNWK623) Description: ICTNWK663 focuses on implementing and managing cloud infrastructure customized for enterprise environments. Students explore various cloud deployment models, including public, private, and hybrid clouds, and acquire practical skills in deploying, configuring, and optimizing cloud services to fulfill the specific requirements of large-scale organizations.

 

Course Title: Building Dynamic Websites (ICTWEB501) Description: ICTWEB555 focuses on the development of dynamic websites, with an emphasis on creating interactive, responsive, and user-friendly web experiences. Students learn various techniques and technologies for building dynamic web content, including server-side scripting languages such as PHP, ASP.NET, or Node.js, as well as client-side scripting languages like JavaScript. Topics covered include database integration, user authentication, content management systems (CMS), and web application frameworks.

 

Course Title: Managing Security, Privacy, and Compliance for Cloud Service Deployments (ICTNWK616) Description: ICTNWK444 focuses on managing security, privacy, and compliance aspects specifically tailored for cloud service deployments. It explores various strategies and methodologies essential for ensuring the security and privacy of data stored and processed in cloud environments, while also adhering to regulatory compliance requirements. Topics covered include risk assessment, encryption techniques, access control mechanisms, data governance, and relevant regulatory frameworks.
